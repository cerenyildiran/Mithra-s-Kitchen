About Us

Welcome to Mithra's Kitchen, where taste and a warm atmosphere meet. Our journey began in 1985 when a couple, passionate about cooking with love, decided to share their culinary creations with the world. Their dream was not just to serve delicious meals but to create a place where guests could feel right at home. Starting as a small family business, our restaurant gradually gained a reputation for its flavors and hospitality, growing into what it is today. For over 30 years, we have continued to serve with the same passion and care. Every dish on our menu has a story behind it, and every recipe is crafted with great effort.

At Mithra's Kitchen, we are open every day of the week to serve you. Our doors are open from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, offering you the opportunity to start your day with a delightful breakfast, enjoy a pleasant lunch with loved ones, or hold your business meetings in a comfortable setting.
