Fish Menu

Salmon pan-fried

Salmon pan-fried in butter is a dish where salmon fillets are cooked in a rich, golden butter until crispy on the outside and tender inside. It is served with creamy mashed potatoes, creating a flavorful and comforting meal.

Fish and fries

Fish and fries is a classic meal with a crispy, fried fish fillet and golden French fries, often served with tartar sauce and lemon wedges.

Cod pan-fried

Cod pan-fried in butter features a tender, flaky fish fillet cooked in rich, golden butter until crispy. It’s served with a side of vegetables, such as carrots, bell peppers, or green beans, creating a balanced and flavorful meal.

Trout in Tomato Sauce with Roasted Cherry Tomatoes

Trout in tomato sauce served with roasted cherry tomatoes features tender trout fillets cooked in a savory tomato sauce, complemented by sweet, caramelized cherry tomatoes. This dish combines the rich flavors of the tomato sauce with the bright, roasted notes of the cherry tomatoes.

Steamed Black Bean Basa Fish

Steamed basa fish with black bean sauce features tender fish cooked with a savory black bean sauce, offering a flavorful and moist dish.

Grilled salmon

Grilled salmon is a dish where salmon fillets are cooked over a grill, giving them a smoky flavor and crispy exterior while keeping the inside tender and juicy. It’s often seasoned with herbs and spices or served with a fresh squeeze of lemon.
